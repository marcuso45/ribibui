Administration & coordination

The administration and coordination section consists of two members, which are also the founders of the project:
Bernd Hoffmann, Federal Office for Radiation Protection, Berlin, Germany.
Bernd Hoffmann got a PhD in Physics and work as a scientist at the German Federal Office for Radiation Protection. He is the head of the section "Radon" as well as of the section "Natural occurring radioactive materials and residues". Since almost 20 years, he is specialized in the fields of Radon and radioactivity in building materials and advice the German government about strategies, techniques and solutions. He is working in corresponding national and European standardization committees.
 His main duties in this project are:

  • Planning of project infrastructure and time schedule
  • Coordination of the various sections
  • Modelling and Simulation
